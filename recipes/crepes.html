Crepes

A West African Delight

Indulge in the light and airy goodness of French crepes. These thin, delicate pancakes are a culinary marvel,
known for their versatility and delectable taste. Whether filled with sweet Nutella and fresh berries or
savory ham and cheese, each bite offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Whisk the flour and eggs.
  2. Gradually add the milk and water.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
