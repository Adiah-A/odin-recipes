Egusi Soup Recipe

Egusi Soup

A West African Delight

Discover the deliciousness of egusi soup, a cherished West African delight. Made with ground melon seeds, fresh
vegetables, and flavorful spices, this soup offers a comforting and satisfying experience. Treat yourself to
a bowl of this traditional African dish and savor the authentic flavors of West Africa.

Ingredients

  • Egusi Seeds
  • Garlic
  • paprika
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Crayfish
  • Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Beef
  • Beef tripe

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, boil the beef seasoned with salt, smoked paprika, pepper, and onions.
  2. Make sure you add plenty of water, so you have about 3-4 cups of beef stock for the soup.
  3. Remove the beef and reserve the stock.
  4. Heat the oil in a heavy saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Add the onions and saut√© until they are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the beef, smoked turkey, and crayfish, and continue cooking.
  7. Stir in the tomatoes with their juices, garlic, and 1-2 cups of beef stock.
  8. Bring to a boil and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  9. Add ground egusi and simmer on medium heat for approximately ten more minutes.
  10. Add beef stock as needed to get the desired consistency or prevent burning.
  11. Finally, throw in the spinach and let it simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  12. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  13. Serve warm with fried plantains, fufu, yams, or your favorite starchy side dish.
